One of the best places to find any Covid Resource –

  • 1. Goto Twitter
    2. Paste in the search window  verified region covid_resource -“not verified” -“unverified” -“needed” -“required”
  • 3. Goto Latest section and search for resource

For example: For finding ICU beds in Delhi,
Search for
verified delhi (bed OR beds OR icu OR oxygen OR ventilator OR ventilators) -“not verified” -“unverified” -“needed” -“required”
